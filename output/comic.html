The Story of Momotaro: The Peach Boy's Journey

Old Woman:

Oh! What a colossal peach! I must retrieve it for my husband!

Momotaro (baby):

Do not be afraid. The Heavens have sent me to be your son.

Momotaro:

I must go to Onigashima. I will defeat the Oni and bring back the stolen treasures to help our people.

Old Woman:

Oh, my son... Onigashima is a dangerous place!

Old Man:

But your heart is true, Momotaro. We are proud.

Old Woman:

These Kibi-dango will give you the strength of a hundred men. Be brave, my son.

Momotaro:

I will return victorious, Father, Mother.

Spotted Dog:

Woof! Such power! I pledge my service to you, Momotaro!

Monkey:

Such a taste! Lead the way, Momotaro! I'm in!

Pheasant:

A powerful gift! Consider me your eyes in the sky, Momotaro!

Momotaro:

With unity, we are strong. To Onigashima!

Oni Minion:

Aargh! My eye!

Monkey:

Heh heh! Simpletons!

Momotaro:

Now!

Oni King:

Please! Spare me! I promise... no more raids! No more stealing!

Momotaro:

Your reign of terror ends today, Oni King! Return all that you have stolen!

Old Woman:

My son! You've done it!

Momotaro:

The Oni are defeated. Our village is safe once more! And the story shall be told for generations...
