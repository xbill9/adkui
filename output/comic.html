The Story of Momotaro: Peach Boy Hero

Baby Momotaro: Don't be afraid. The Heavens have sent me to be your son.

Old Woman: A son! A miracle!

Momotaro: I must go to Onigashima. I will defeat the Oni and bring back what they have stolen.

Old Woman: Take these Kibi-dango, my son. They say one gives the strength of a hundred men.

Momotaro: Friend, would you care for a taste of this Kibi-dango?

Monkey: OOH-OOH-AHH! GIMME! GIMME!

Momotaro: We are a team. And together, we are unstoppable.

Oni Guard: OUCH! My eyes! What in the-?!

Monkey: OOOH-HOO-HOO! This way, friends!

Oni King: STOP! MERCY! I beg you, Momotaro! I surrender!

Momotaro: You will trouble no one ever again, Oni King. Swear it.

Villager 1: MOMOTARO! OUR HERO!

Old Woman: My son! My brave, brave son!

Elder (Storyteller): ...and so, Momotaro, the Peach Boy, lived out his days as a legendary protector, reminding us all that courage and cooperation can overcome even the greatest evils!

Child 2: I want to be like Momotaro!
